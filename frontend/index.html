About ExoDetect AI

ExoDetect AI is an advanced machine learning platform designed to automatically identify exoplanets from NASA mission data. The platform utilizes multiple algorithms including XGBoost, Random Forest, Neural Networks, and SVM trained on extensive datasets from the Kepler, K2, and TESS missions to classify planetary candidates with high accuracy.

Detection Method

The platform uses the transit method, detecting the slight dimming of starlight when a planet passes between a star and our satellites. By analyzing various parameters such as orbital period, transit duration, and planetary radius, the AI model can distinguish between confirmed exoplanets, candidates, and false positives.
